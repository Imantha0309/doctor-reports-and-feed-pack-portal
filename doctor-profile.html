About Dr. Sarah Johnson

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a highly experienced cardiologist with over 15 years of dedicated service in cardiovascular medicine. She completed her medical degree from Harvard Medical School and specialized in interventional cardiology at Johns Hopkins Hospital.

Her expertise includes the diagnosis and treatment of coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and preventive cardiology. Dr. Johnson is known for her compassionate approach to patient care and her commitment to staying updated with the latest advances in cardiovascular medicine.
